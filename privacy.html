1. Overview

Speak With Clarity ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website for our communication masterclass event held at the Main Auditorium, University of Lagos, Akoka, Yaba, Lagos, Nigeria on October 17, 2026. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect any personal information you provide when using our website or registering for the event.

By accessing our website or submitting a registration form, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Identity Data: First name, last name, title, and role.
  • Contact Data: Email address, phone number, and WhatsApp number.
  • Professional Data: Organisation or institution name, professional bio, and session topic.
  • Accessibility Data: Dietary requirements and accessibility needs (collected only to accommodate your attendance).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and cookie data collected automatically when you visit our website.

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as financial information, government IDs, or health records beyond accessibility needs you voluntarily provide.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use personal information for the following purposes:

  • To confirm and manage your registration as a moderator, speaker, or attendee.
  • To communicate event logistics, schedule updates, and pre-event instructions.
  • To accommodate dietary and accessibility requirements at the event.
  • To issue certificates of participation.
  • To compile anonymised statistics about attendance and engagement.
  • To contact you about future Speak With Clarity events, only where you have consented.

We will never use your personal data for purposes other than those described above without your explicit consent.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share limited data with:

  • Event partners and sponsors — only aggregated, anonymised data unless you opt-in to be contacted directly.
  • Service providers — third-party tools we use to operate the website (e.g., email delivery services) under strict confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal authorities — where required by Nigerian law or a valid court order.

Any service provider we engage is contractually obligated to process your data only on our instructions and to keep it secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy:

  • Registration data is retained for up to 12 months after the event to handle any post-event queries or certificate requests.
  • Technical and analytics data may be retained for up to 6 months.
  • If you request deletion of your data, we will comply within 30 days, subject to any legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction: Ask us to limit how we use your data.
  • Objection: Object to our use of your data for marketing purposes.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@speakwithclarity.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

Our website uses a limited number of cookies to improve your experience:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic site functionality (e.g., theme preference).
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the site.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function as expected. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Our website uses HTTPS encryption, and access to registration data is restricted to authorised team members only.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. If you believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

  • Email: info@speakwithclarity.com
  • Address: Speak With Clarity, Main Auditorium, University of Lagos, Akoka, Yaba, Lagos, Nigeria

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated via email to registered participants. The "Effective date" at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.
