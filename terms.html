2. Event Description

Speak With Clarity is a one-day intensive communication masterclass facilitated by David Egbeyemi, covering three core pillars:

  • Pillar 1 — Psychology of Confidence: Overcoming speech anxiety and building a confident mindset.
  • Pillar 2 — Thought Structuring: Frameworks for logical, persuasive, on-the-spot communication.
  • Pillar 3 — Executive Presence & Modulation: Voice control, body language, and commanding delivery.

The event includes live sessions, interactive workshops, a networking lunch, a live feedback lab, and an awards/certificate ceremony. The programme runs from 8:30 AM to 6:00 PM WAT.
