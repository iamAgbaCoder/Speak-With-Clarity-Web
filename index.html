Event Schedule

A single, intensive day packed with structured lessons, interactive workshops, and high-impact speaking exercises.

08:30 AM - 09:00 AM Pre-Event

Registration & Welcome Coffee

Lobby check-in, handbook collection, and informal meet-up before the workshop begins.

09:00 AM - 10:30 AM Pillar 1 Session

The Psychology of Speech Confidence

Led by David Egbeyemi. Overcoming stage fright, decoding the body's anxiety signals, and building a solid mindset for speaking under pressure.

10:30 AM - 11:00 AM Break

Coffee Break & Networking

Refreshments served in the courtyard. Meet fellow participants and exchange thoughts.

11:00 AM - 01:00 PM Pillar 2 Session

Thought Structuring & Precision Delivery

Led by David Egbeyemi. Transitioning from unstructured thoughts to persuasive speaking. Learn frameworks like PREP and the Pyramid Principle to build coherent speeches on the fly.

01:00 PM - 02:00 PM Lunch

Catered Networking Lunch

Enjoy a premium buffet lunch while connecting with industry peers and event sponsors.

02:00 PM - 03:30 PM Pillar 3 Session

Vocal Modulation & Executive Presence

Led by David Egbeyemi. Mechanics of speech: tone control, volume shifts, breathing techniques, and posture correction to guarantee maximum impact in any boardroom.

03:30 PM - 05:00 PM Interactive Lab

Live Feedback Lab & Speech Simulations

Led by David Egbeyemi. Interactive breakout session where participants deliver short speeches and receive personalized coaching and corrective notes in real time.

05:00 PM - 06:00 PM Closing

Awarding of Certificates & Cocktail Reception

Distribution of the certificates of completion followed by cocktails, refreshments, and a networking session on the terrace.
